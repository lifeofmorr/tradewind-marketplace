## Tips for a great bug report

- **Reproduce it before you report it.** If you can't reproduce, say so explicitly — intermittent bugs are still worth reporting.
- **One bug per report.** Don't bundle five issues into one form; we'll lose track.
- **Screenshots beat descriptions.** A 5-second screen recording is even better.
- **Include the URL.** "It broke on the listing page" is hard to act on; `/listings/abc-123-xyz` is not.
- **Note what you were doing right before the bug.** Often the trigger is the previous action, not the page you're on when it appears.
