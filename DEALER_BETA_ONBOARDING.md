## Step-by-Step Dealer Onboarding (what they'll do once they click the link)

1. **Click the signup link** — `?role=dealer` pre-fills the role so they don't pick the wrong path.
2. **Create the account** — email, password, and dealership name.
3. **Fill in dealership info** — address, phone, website, specialties (boats / cars / trucks / mixed), years in business.
4. **Add the first listing** — boat, car, or truck. Encourage them to try the AI listing generator if available; otherwise the manual form takes 2–3 minutes.
5. **Upload photos** — at least one, ideally 4–6 for full visual coverage.
6. **Explore the dashboard** — show them the Inventory Health card and Dealer Analytics so they see what they'll get over time.
7. **Set up the lead follow-up assistant** — AI suggestions appear once they have inquiries; in the empty state they can preview a sample.
8. **Submit feedback** — once they've spent 30+ minutes in the product, point them to the feedback form.

## Tips for the inviter

- **Personalize the first sentence.** Mention their dealership by name and what makes them a good fit (specialty, region, reputation).
- **Set the right expectation about volume.** Be upfront that this is a private beta — they won't see hundreds of buyers on day one, but the AI tools work right now.
- **Offer a 15-min screen share.** A real conversation closes more dealers than a polished email.
- **Follow up at day 3 and day 7.** Most beta drop-off happens because nobody nudged.
