# TradeWind · Customer Acquisition Playbook

A field manual for the first 90 days. Real scripts, real templates,
real follow-up cadences. Copy/paste, swap names, send.

**Posture**: TradeWind is the *premium*, AI-native alternative to YachtWorld
+ Boats.com + Boat Trader + Cars.com — for serious sellers and serious
buyers. Lean into "fewer, better listings, fewer tire-kickers, every lead
fraud-screened." Don't compete on price; compete on quality.

**One-line pitch**: *"TradeWind is the AI-powered marketplace for boats,
autos, dealers, and serious buyers. We screen every inquiry for fraud
before it hits your inbox, and our AI writes the listings for you."*

**The first ask** (always the same): *"Can I list 5 of your boats / cars
on TradeWind for free for the next 6 months? You keep every lead. If you
ever ask, I take them down. Worst case it's free traffic."*

## 1. Dealer outreach (boats)

### 1a. Cold call script — boat dealer / brokerage

> "Hi, this is **[name]** with **TradeWind**. I'm building the new
> AI-powered marketplace for boats and high-end autos — think YachtWorld
> meets a fraud filter. We're hand-picking 25 dealers in **[metro]** to
> seed inventory, and I'd love to put your boats in front of our buyers
> for free for the next six months. No obligation, you keep every lead.
> Got 90 seconds for the gist?"

**If "Yes":**

> "Two things make us different. One — every inquiry that hits your inbox
> has been AI-screened for fraud, so no more 'is this still available?'
> texts from sketchy numbers. Two — we generate the listing copy and
> spec sheet automatically from your existing photos and a one-line
> description. You give me a CSV of your inventory, I do the rest.
> What's a good email to send a 1-page sample listing to so you can
> see the quality?"

**If "Send me an email":** Use the cold email template (1c) and end the
call. Don't pitch over voicemail.

**If "Not interested":**

> "Totally fair. One last thing — even if you never list with us, we run
> a free buyer-side fraud-check tool. If you ever get a sketchy email
> on a boat, paste it in and you'll get a verdict in 5 seconds. Want
> the link?"

(Goal: get them on the list anyway. Send the link later.)

| --------- | -------- |
| *"I already use YachtWorld/Cars.com."* | "Totally — I'd never ask you to leave them. Most of our dealers run TradeWind in addition to their existing channels. Free for 6 months, you keep every lead, low risk." |
| *"What's the catch?"* | "Honestly, none for the first six months. We need launch inventory — we're trading free listings for the trust you give us by being on the platform early. After 6 months, dealer subscriptions start at $99/mo for Starter." |
| *"How do you compete with **[entrenched]**?"* | "We don't, on volume. We compete on lead quality — every inquiry is AI-screened for fraud, so you spend zero time on tire-kickers. We also write the listings for you, which YachtWorld/Cars.com don't." |
| *"I don't want my prices public."* | "Totally fine — you can mark a listing 'Price on request' and route inquiries through the form. Buyers still see the unit." |
| *"How many buyers do you actually have?"* | "Honestly, we're new. We launched **[month]**. The early-mover dealers are the ones who'll show up in our buyer-side AI concierge recommendations as we scale. That's the real prize." |
| *"What about exclusive listings?"* | "Not required. List wherever you want. We're better when we're additive." |
| *"Do I have to feed you a CSV?"* | "No — I'll scrape your inventory page myself and send you the drafts to review before going live. Zero work on your end." |
| *"Send me an email"* | (Always say YES, end the call quickly, and follow up within an hour with the email template above. Reference the specific thing they mentioned.) |

## 7. 30-day launch plan

### Week 1 — Foundations + 5 dealers

- **Mon**: Create your TradeWind admin account, write 1 seed blog post,
  1 seed market report, 10 hand-curated featured listings. Verify
  every flow in LAUNCH_CHECKLIST.md Section 9.
- **Tue**: Personally call 10 dealers in your network. Goal: 3 yeses.
- **Wed**: White-glove import the first 3 dealers' inventory. Use the
  AI listing generator. Aim for 30 listings live by end of day.
- **Thu**: Cold call 20 more dealers (boat + auto mix). Goal: 2 yeses.
- **Fri**: White-glove import. End the week with **5 dealers,
  ~50 listings live**.
- **Sat/Sun**: Reach out to 30 private sellers via DM (Facebook
  Marketplace, Craigslist). Goal: 6 listings.

### Week 2 — Service partners + private sellers

- **Mon**: Email blast to 30 marine mechanics + 20 surveyors in FL/SC.
  Use the email templates. Goal: 5 partners.
- **Tue**: Email blast to 25 transport companies. Goal: 3 partners.
- **Wed**: Reach out to 5 marine insurance brokers and 5 marine
  lenders. Pick **one** of each as your launch preferred partner.
- **Thu**: Onboard 10 more private sellers. End the week with
  **~80 listings**.
- **Fri**: Submit `/sitemap.xml` to Google Search Console + Bing.
  Configure GSC alerts for crawl errors.
- **Sat/Sun**: Plant 10 reviews across your launch dealers. Honest
  but enthusiastic — "Bought my Whaler from Outpost in 3 weeks,
  zero drama" type.

### Week 3 — Marketing flywheel

- **Mon**: Write 4 blog posts (one per category): center-console
  buyer's guide, exotic financing 101, marine PPI checklist, RV
  road-trip planning. Use the AI to rough-draft, polish by hand.
- **Tue**: Write 1 market report (Q-current center-console market).
  Plant graphs from your seed data.
- **Wed**: Instagram + TikTok content — reels of the listings, drone
  shots of featured boats.
- **Thu**: Outreach to boat-content YouTubers — offer affiliate links
  on 3 of them. 10% of any closed concierge deal.
- **Fri**: First Stripe transaction. Buy your own Featured listing
  upgrade with a real card to verify the flow end-to-end.

### Week 4 — Scale + first revenue

- **Mon**: Cold-call 50 more dealers. By now you have referenceable
  dealers — name-drop them.
- **Tue**: First paid Featured listings — pitch your existing dealers
  to feature their best inventory ($49 each, 30 days).
- **Wed**: First Dealer Pro subscription. Pitch your 5 launch dealers:
  "Free trial ends in 30 days, lock in $99/mo before Pro tier increases
  to $249."
- **Thu**: First concierge engagement. You'll get a buyer who's serious.
  $499 upfront. Run them through the AI concierge intake.
- **Fri**: Audit the first month — where are inquiries coming from?
  Which categories convert? Which dealers are getting messaged?
- **Sat/Sun**: Catch up on customer support. Reach out to every
  buyer who messaged a listing in week 1–4 and ask for feedback.

## 8. KPIs to track from day 1

| Metric | Week 1 target | Week 4 target | Where |
| ------ | ------------- | ------------- | ----- |
| Active listings | 30 | 200 | `/admin` overview |
| Buyer signups (cumulative) | 25 | 250 | `/admin/users` |
| Inquiries / listing | 0.3 | 1.5 | `/admin/listings` |
| Featured purchases | 0 | 5 | `/admin/payments` |
| Dealer subscriptions | 0 | 3 | `/admin/payments` |
| Concierge engagements | 0 | 1 | `/admin/requests` |
| Open fraud flags > 24h | 0 | 0 | `/admin/fraud` |

If the inquiries/listing ratio stays below 0.3 by week 4, you have a
demand problem (not enough buyer marketing). Double down on SEO
content + paid Google search ads on `[boat type] for sale [state]`
queries.

If active listings stay below 100 by week 4, you have a supply
problem. Hire a part-time inventory ops person ($20/hr × 20 hr/week)
to white-glove imports.

## 9. Top 5 first-revenue actions

1. **Sign your first 5 launch dealers, free.** Inventory is everything.
2. **White-glove import their listings within 48 hours of saying yes.**
   Show, don't tell.
3. **Plant 10 truly featured listings on the homepage.** Visual
   density signals "real marketplace" to first-time visitors.
4. **Charge yourself $49 for a Featured listing** — verifies the
   Stripe webhook end-to-end and gives you a real first transaction.
5. **Offer the first 3 Dealer Pro subscriptions at half off** ($49/mo
   for 6 months) to your launch dealers — converts free trials into
   recurring revenue and gives you 3 testimonials for the next 50
   pitches.

The order matters. Skip step 1 and steps 2–5 don't matter.

## 10. Long-term moat (after you have 1,000 listings)

- **Buyer-side AI concierge** becomes the lock-in. Once buyers tell
  the AI what they want (50+ data points: budget, intended use,
  must-haves), they don't want to start over on YachtWorld.
- **Verified dealer ratings** — once you have 100+ reviews, that
  social proof is hard for incumbents to replicate.
- **Programmatic SEO** — by month 6, every state × brand × city
  combo should rank top-3 for "boats for sale in [state]" longtail
  queries. That's free, defensible traffic.
- **Cross-category buyer profiles** — someone who bought a boat is
  3× more likely to buy a truck or RV. Cross-sell concierge
  opportunities the incumbents can't touch.
