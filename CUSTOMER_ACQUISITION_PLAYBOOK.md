# TradeWind · Customer Acquisition Playbook

> "The AI-powered marketplace for boats, autos, dealers, and serious buyers."
> Practical, copy-paste outreach for the first 90 days. Replace `{{Name}}`,
> `{{Dealership}}`, `{{Your Name}}`, `{{Phone}}`, `{{Calendar Link}}` before
> sending. URL placeholder: `https://gotradewind.com`.

Tone notes:
- Specific over generic. Mention something on their site / inventory.
- Lead with what they get, not what you want.
- Pricing hidden in the first email. Only tease "free for early dealers."
- Phone first; email is for follow-up. Voicemails get returned at 3-5%; cold email at 1-2%.

## Part 5 — Cold call script with objection handling

Open with a permission-based pattern interrupt. Then qualify, demo, close.

### Open
> "Hi {{Name}}, this is {{Your Name}} from TradeWind. I know you weren't
> expecting my call — got 27 seconds for the why?"

(Wait. ~70% will say yes. The 30% who say no, thank them and move on.)

### Why
> "We're an AI-powered marketplace for boats and cars. Our pitch is dead
> simple: instead of you spending 30 minutes writing each listing, the AI
> drafts it in 30 seconds. We're onboarding the first 25 dealers in
> {{State}} for free through {{date}}. Are you spending time on listings
> right now or has someone in the back office got it covered?"

### Qualify
- Who writes their listings now?
- How many do they post per month?
- What sites are they on (BoatTrader, Boats.com, YachtWorld, CarGurus, AutoTrader)?
- Average days-to-sell?

### Demo offer
> "Want to see the AI tool work on one of your boats live? Takes 5
> minutes — I'll screen-share, paste in one of your existing listings,
> and you can compare what the AI writes to what you have."

### Close
> "Two options for {{date}}: Tuesday at 10 or Thursday at 2 — what
> works?"

(Always offer two specific times, never "when's good?" Specifics
double the booking rate.)

### Objections — full set

**"What does it cost?"**
> "$99/mo for Starter, $249 for Pro, $599 for Premier. But the first
> 25 dealers in {{State}} get 6 months free. The free tier is real, no
> card."

**"We already have a website."**
> "Right — we're not replacing your website. We're an additional channel
> like BoatTrader. Your website handles people who already know you;
> we bring you people who don't."

**"How does this compare to BoatTrader / YachtWorld / CarGurus?"**
> "Three differences. One: AI writes your listings. Two: every inquiry
> is scored for buyer quality before it hits your inbox. Three: we're
> a fraction of their price. We're not trying to replace them at year
> five — at month one, we just want to be the third or fourth channel
> you use."

**"How will you get buyers?"**
> "Honest answer: we're seeding 100+ listings in {{State}} this month
> and running paid social and SEO from week one. Our pricing assumes
> we're proving lead volume in the first 90 days — that's why dealers
> are free until then."

**"I need to think about it / send me info."**
> "Of course. Two questions before I let you go: are you the right
> person for this decision, or should I include someone else on the
> email? And what's the one thing that would have to be true for this
> to be a 'yes' for you?"

**"Send me a follow-up email."**
> "Will do. While I have you — what's your best-selling category right
> now? I'll line up two real leads we already have in that category and
> send them with the email."

**"How do I know you'll still be here in a year?"**
> "Fair. Starter has no contract, cancel any time. Pro and Premier are
> month-to-month too. If we go away, you're out a few months of $99 —
> not a big bet."

**"We tried something like this before and it didn't work."**
> "What was the deal-breaker? … Got it. Here's how we're different on
> that point: {{address it directly}}."

## Part 6 — 30-day launch plan

A concrete week-by-week with daily tactics. Assumes 2 people — you and
maybe one BD partner.

### Week 1 — Inventory + foundation

**Goal**: 50 listings live, 3 dealers paying (or in free trial), social
profiles active.

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Mon | Personally seed 10 listings from your network (friends with boats/cars). Photograph yourself if needed. |
| Tue | Set up Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn company pages. Profile pic = TradeWind logo. Link in bio = `gotradewind.com`. Post intro video (60 sec, you on camera, "Hi, I'm launching TradeWind"). |
| Wed | Reach out to 10 dealers in your state by phone. Goal: 3 demo calls booked. Use script from Part 5. |
| Thu | Run demo calls. Onboard 2-3 to free Starter plan. Help them upload 5 listings each. |
| Fri | Seed 20 more listings (free dealers from Thursday). End week: ~50 listings live. |
| Sat-Sun | Soft email blast to your personal network: "TradeWind is live, here's the link." |

### Week 2 — Outreach scale

**Goal**: 50 outreach contacts, 5 more dealers / brokers, first blog posts.

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Mon | Build a list of 100 boat dealers in {{State}} — Yelp + Google Maps + state marine industry directory. CSV: name, dealership, phone, email, website. |
| Tue | Day-1 cold call blitz: 30 calls. Goal: 5 demo bookings. Voicemails count — leave them with `gotradewind.com`. |
| Wed | Day-1 cold email blitz: send 50 emails using template from 1.1 / 1.2. |
| Thu | Demo calls (rolling). Run Day-3 follow-up emails. |
| Fri | Publish 2 blog posts in `/admin/blog`: "Best boats under $50k for 2026" and "How to spot a fraud listing." Both keyword-targeted. |
| Sat-Sun | Post 3 of the best listings to r/boats and r/cars (NOT promotional — just photos with a caption). Drives organic search traffic. |

### Week 3 — Service providers + Stripe activation

**Goal**: 10 service providers onboarded, Stripe payments live, first
featured listings sold.

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Mon | Switch Stripe from test → live (LAUNCH_CHECKLIST.md step 15). Run full smoke test before going public. |
| Tue | Cold-call 20 marine service providers (mechanics, surveyors, detailers) in your state. Use script 1.5. |
| Wed | Cold-call 10 transport companies. Use script 1.6. |
| Thu | Email the 50 dealers who didn't reply in Week 2 — Day-7 follow-up. |
| Fri | Push the 5 best dealers to buy a Featured listing ($49). At this point you have buyer traffic. |
| Sat-Sun | First retargeting / paid social campaign — start with $50/day on Meta, target boat/car shoppers in {{State}}. |

### Week 4 — PR + auctions + reviews

**Goal**: Local press coverage, first auction running, 10+ reviews
collected, 100 total listings.

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Mon | PR push: pitch local business journals + state boating magazines. Hook: "AI-powered marketplace launches in {{State}} — first 25 dealers join free." Personalize 5-10 pitches. |
| Tue | Reach out to 3 micro-influencers in the boat/car space (5-50k followers on Instagram). Offer: free Featured listing slot for them or their network in exchange for a story. |
| Wed | Launch first auction. Pick a unique boat from a friendly seller. Promote heavily on social. Use it as a case study after. |
| Thu | Email every buyer who's submitted an inquiry asking for a review of the dealer they contacted. Script: "How did {{Dealer}} treat you? 30-second review here." |
| Fri | Day-14 break-up emails to dealers who never replied. Expect ~10% reply. |
| Sat-Sun | Publish "Month 1 in numbers" post on LinkedIn (your personal). Founder narrative + listings count + auction result. Drives founder credibility. |

### Metrics to track from day 1

Spreadsheet, updated daily:
- Listings live (target: 50 by end of W1, 100 by end of W4)
- Dealers signed up (target: 3 by W1, 10 by W4)
- Service providers signed up (target: 10 by W3)
- Inquiries submitted per day (target: 10/day by W4)
- Stripe MRR (target: $500 MRR by W4 if dealer pricing kicks in early)
- Cold calls made (target: 30/day on outreach days)
- Cold emails sent (target: 50/day on outreach days)
- Reply rate (target: 5%+ on emails, 15%+ on calls)
- Demo-to-trial conversion (target: 50%)
- Trial-to-paid conversion (target: 30% by month 3)

## Part 8 — First-revenue fast wins (next 7 days)

If you only do 5 things this week, do these:

1. **Sell 3 Featured listings ($49 × 3 = $147)** to dealers who already
   have inventory in the seed data. Email them: "We're putting your
   {{boat}} on the homepage carousel for 30 days for $49 — first 5 slots
   only."
2. **Onboard 1 paid dealer** ($99/mo) — pick the dealer most likely to
   say yes from your Week 1 conversations. Skip the free trial; offer
   "first month $49, then $99/mo" instead.
3. **Run 1 paid Concierge engagement ($499)** — find a friend or
   network contact who's actively shopping for a $50k+ boat. Walk them
   through `/concierge`, run the AI intake, take the $499 manually if
   needed.
4. **Publish 1 Sponsored Market Report** ($500-1000 sponsorship from a
   lender or insurance partner). Use `/admin/market-reports` and slap
   their logo on it.
5. **Sell a Premier dealer plan ($599/mo)** to one big-name dealer in
   your state. Offer: "First quarter at $299/mo, then $599. We'll do a
   joint announcement post on LinkedIn."

Total target this week: $1,500–$3,000 in first revenue. It's not the
business — it's the proof that the business can take money.
