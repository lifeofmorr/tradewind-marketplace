## What TradeWind is

A premium marketplace and transaction platform for high-value assets — boats,
autos, and aircraft — and the dealers, brokers, and service providers who
move them.

---

## Who it's for

- **Boat and yacht dealers / brokers** — list inventory, capture buyer requests,
  coordinate transactions.
- **Auto dealers** (exotic, classic, specialty) — differentiated listings with
  condition history and market comparison.
- **Aircraft brokers** — specs, airframe logs, pre-buy coordination, aviation
  services.
- **Service providers** — marine shops, aviation maintenance, inspection firms,
  detailers, transport, ferry pilots.
- **Lenders, insurance, and escrow partners** — receive qualified deal leads
  with asset context already attached.
- **Serious buyers** — verified asset data, transparent pricing, no cold calls.

## What's live today

- 65 demo listings across boats, autos, and aircraft
- Asset Passport — condition history, maintenance records, inspection results
- Deal Score — market comparison baked into every listing
- True Cost estimate — financing, insurance, and registration factored in
- Dealer tools — listing management, buyer activity, inquiry threads
- AI listing assistant — generate structured listing detail from photos and notes
- Transaction Rooms — shared deal space for buyer, seller, and service partners
- 17 edge functions powering real-time search, matching, and notifications
- /beta, /feedback, and /how-it-works live and accessible

---

## Why TradeWind is different

- Built for the **full transaction**, not just listings — from inquiry to closed deal.
- The only platform with a dedicated **aircraft vertical** alongside boats and autos.
- **Transaction Rooms** replace email chains for multi-party deal coordination.
- Trust infrastructure baked in — Asset Passport, Deal Score, verified sellers —
  so buyers don't need to hedge before flying across the country to see a boat.
- Not a lead aggregator. Not a classifieds site. A transaction platform.
