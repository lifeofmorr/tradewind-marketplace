# Outreach Sequence Library

13 vertical-specific sequences. Each sequence contains:
- **Email 1** (first contact)
- **Follow-up 1** (+3 days, no reply)
- **Follow-up 2** (+7 days from FU1, still no reply)
- **Final close-the-loop** (+14 days from FU2)
- **LinkedIn connection note** (under 300 chars)
- **LinkedIn DM** (after they connect)
- **Instagram DM** (only if their IG is active and business-public)
- **Phone script** (≤60 sec)
- **Voicemail script** (≤25 sec)

All written in Don's voice (`HUMAN_VOICE_RULES.md`). Replace bracketed tokens.

The close-the-loop is mandatory. It exits cleanly with a no-pressure door open, and almost always pulls a real reply.

## Universal rules across all sequences

- **Send Email 1 only after the lead is in the CRM, scored 3+, and has a real personalization angle.**
- **All follow-ups are conditional on no reply.** A reply ends the sequence and routes to `REPLY_HANDLING_SYSTEM.md`.
- **A negative reply or opt-out** flips Do Not Contact = Yes and exits the sequence.
- **The close-the-loop is mandatory**, not optional. It's the email that most often gets a real reply.
- **Maximum 4 emails total per lead.** After the close-the-loop, no more email — they go to a 6-month nurture park.
- **No simultaneous channels in week 1.** Don't email and LinkedIn-DM and call on the same day. Email first, LinkedIn at FU2 if quiet, phone at the close-the-loop if Score 5.
