# TradeWind 2-Minute Walkthrough Script

**Owner:** Don Morrison
**Date:** 2026-05-27
**Format:** Loom, selfie-cam + screen, under 2:15.
**Tone:** Founder-led, natural, not salesy. One human talking to another.

---

## Opening (0:00 – 0:15)

> "Hey, this is Don. I'm building TradeWind as a premium marketplace and
> transaction platform for boats, autos, aircraft, and the service providers
> around them."

**Show:** Your face briefly, then switch to screen sharing `/beta`.

---

## What it is (0:15 – 0:35)

> "The core idea is simple: there's no platform today built specifically for
> high-value asset transactions. Not just listings — the whole transaction.
> Pre-buys, financing, escrow, insurance, inspections. TradeWind connects all
> of that in one place."

**Show:** Homepage hero. Point to the vertical navigation (boats, autos, aircraft).

---

## Who it's for (0:35 – 0:50)

> "We're building this for dealers, brokers, and service providers who are
> tired of duct-taping together listings, emails, and calls to close a single
> deal. And for buyers who want to trust what they're looking at before they
> fly across the country to see it."

**Show:** The vertical toggle. Land on `/boats` or `/aircraft`.

---

## Beta (0:50 – 1:00)

> "This is live beta — not a mockup. You can visit it right now at
> tradewind-marketplace.vercel.app/beta. The inventory is curated for demo,
> with real listings from seed partners coming in."

**Show:** Navigate to `/beta`. Point to the beta access section.

---

## Marketplace categories (1:00 – 1:15)

> "We have boats, autos, and aircraft live today. Each vertical has category
> filters, condition, price, and the kinds of details that actually matter —
> hull type, engine hours, maintenance records."

**Show:** Browse listings briefly. Click into one listing.

---

## Asset Passport + Deal Score + True Cost (1:15 – 1:40)

> "Every listing has what we call an Asset Passport — condition history,
> maintenance records, inspection results — and a Deal Score that tells a buyer
> how this compares to market. We also surface a True Cost estimate that
> factors in financing, insurance, and registration, so there are no surprises
> at the end."

**Show:** Listing detail page. Point to the Asset Passport panel, Deal Score
badge, and True Cost breakdown.

---

## Transaction Room (1:40 – 1:52)

> "When a buyer gets serious, a Transaction Room opens — a shared space for
> the buyer, seller, and any service providers to coordinate offers, documents,
> pre-buys, and closing. No more email chains."

**Show:** The Transaction Room UI or demo screenshot.

---

## Dealer + service value (1:52 – 2:05)

> "For dealers and service providers, it's about qualified buyers and clean
> workflow — not just a listing portal. You can see buyer requests, manage
> inquiry threads, and get matched to deals in your vertical."

**Show:** Dealer-side view or `/admin` preview. Briefly mention service
provider lead flow.

---

## Close + ask (2:05 – 2:15)

> "I'm not opening this wide yet. I'm looking for honest feedback from a
> small group of people in the space before we push it further. If you're
> in boats, autos, aircraft, or services, I'd genuinely like to hear what
> you think.
>
> There's a short feedback form at `/feedback`, or you can book a 10-minute
> call with me directly on the beta page. Either way — no pitch, just a
> conversation."

**Show:** Navigate to `/beta`. Point to the CTA buttons. End on screen.

---

## Notes for recording

- Keep camera on for the opening 15 seconds, then screen-share the rest.
- Record at 1080p. No intro music. No outro.
- If you stumble on a line, pause 2 seconds and restart the sentence — editing
  is easier than re-recording.
- Upload unlisted to Loom. Paste the link into outreach and `/beta` as needed.
