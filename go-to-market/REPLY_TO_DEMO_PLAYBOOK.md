# Reply-to-Demo Playbook

**Owner:** Don Morrison (don@lifeofmorr.com)
**Date:** 2026-05-27
**Status:** Active — use for every inbound reply on the TradeWind 100 campaign.

The TradeWind 100 outreach is going out one personal email at a time. When a
reply lands, the goal is the same as the original message: be a real person,
respect the recipient's time, never oversell, and convert the conversation into
a 10-minute demo or an honest "not right now."

These 20 responses are designed to be copy-paste ready. Edit the first name and
the one specific detail; do not edit the tone.

---

## How to use this playbook

1. Classify the reply into one of the 20 scenarios.
2. Copy the matching response.
3. Replace `[FIRST]`, `[COMPANY]`, `[SPECIFIC]`, and `[CALENDAR_LINK]`.
4. Send from `don@lifeofmorr.com` — same thread, same signature, no template
   variables visible.
5. Log the reply on `/admin/outreach` (Replies tab) and advance the lead in
   the beta pipeline when relevant.

**Voice rules** (these override any template that drifts):

- One sentence under 25 words wherever possible.
- Premium calm, never breathless. No emojis. No exclamation points except in
  greetings.
- No invented traction ("hundreds of dealers love it"). No fake urgency.
- Founder voice, signed Don. Treat them like a peer.
- Never promise features that aren't shipped today on `/beta`.
- Always offer a way out (no follow-up, opt-out) when they hesitate.
