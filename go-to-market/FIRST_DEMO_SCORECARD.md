## Scoring Guide

**5 — Strongly yes / very clear / no hesitation**
**4 — Leans yes, minor reservations**
**3 — Neutral or genuinely unsure**
**2 — Leans no, open to being convinced**
**1 — Strongly no / very confused / firm objection**

For the overall score, average the four reaction scores (would use, would list, would refer, would pay). Round to one decimal.

- **4.0–5.0:** Strong beta fit — move fast, send beta invite same day
- **2.5–3.9:** Feedback-only or needs a feature first — valuable but not a near-term active user
- **<2.5:** Likely not a fit — close politely, no aggressive follow-up

4. Choose the matching template from `POST_DEMO_NEXT_STEPS.md` and send within 2 hours
