## Step-by-Step Seller Onboarding

1. **Sign up as a seller** via the `?role=seller` link.
2. **List your boat, car, or truck** — pick the category, fill in the basics (make, model, year, condition, price, location).
3. **Get AI to write your listing** (if available) — paste a few bullet points about the vehicle and the AI writes the description. Edit to taste.
4. **Upload photos** — minimum one, ideally 6–10 across multiple angles. Better photos = more inquiries.
5. **Set the price** — see comparable listings if available. Be realistic; over-priced listings sit.
6. **Publish** — listing goes live after a quick admin review (usually under 2 hours).
7. **Share with buyers** — copy the listing URL and send to anyone you've already been talking to. The link works for buyers without an account.
8. **Track inquiries** — check your dashboard for messages. Reply through TradeWind to keep the conversation logged and to keep your phone number private until you choose to share it.

## Tips for the inviter

- **Lead with their specific vehicle.** "I noticed you've been thinking about selling the {boat/car}" beats a generic invite.
- **Make the AI tool the hook.** Most private sellers hate writing listings. The AI generator is the easiest demo to land.
- **Keep the ask small.** First action is just "post the listing" — feedback can come later.
- **Check in at day 7.** Did they get inquiries? If yes, ask how the conversation went. If no, suggest tweaks (price, photos, title).
