## 4. OBJECTION HANDLING

### "How is this different from BoatTrader / AutoTrader / Controller?"

> "Those are listing boards — they show inventory and generate inquiries. TradeWind is a transaction platform — we power the entire flow from inquiry to close, including the service providers who make the close happen. Listing boards charge you to be seen. We charge you for outcomes."

Long form: BoatTrader / AutoTrader / Controller are 20-year-old classified-ad businesses with bolt-on features. TradeWind was designed AI-native from day one: AI listing assist, AI pricing intelligence, AI concierge, AI lead matching, transaction room. The architectural difference compounds.

### "How do I trust the listings are real?"

> "Three layers. (1) Every seller goes through an admin-approved beta onboarding — no anonymous postings. (2) Listings flagged by our fraud-screen edge function get human review before going live. (3) Buyers see verified-seller badges, listing-history timestamps, and Asset Passports that score listing completeness. We don't claim to catch every bad actor, but we're materially better than open-classified platforms."

### "Do you offer escrow?"

> "Today: integrated escrow is gated to 'Request Access' — we surface escrow partners in the transaction room and route requests to them, but we don't run escrow ourselves. We're actively negotiating with [Escrow.com / specialty escrow firms] and will flip the integration live when contracts are signed. We don't pretend to offer something we don't, and we'd rather lose a deal than mislead a buyer."

### "Can I import my existing dealer inventory?"

> "Yes — CSV import is live today for any dealer. Direct DMS feed integration (DealerSocket, vAuto, etc.) is part of the Dealer Premium tier and is currently set up case-by-case via our team. If your DMS isn't on our list yet, send us a sample feed and we'll wire it in."

### "Aircraft brokers won't switch from Controller."

> "We're not asking them to switch. We're asking them to LIST on us in parallel for one quarter. Most brokers run multi-listing strategies anyway. The brokers who try us tell us they stick because of (a) the transaction room — Controller has no equivalent — and (b) the in-platform service providers, especially escrow agents and pre-buy inspectors. We win on closing, not on impressions."

### "Are these listings real or demo?"

> "Today, the 65 listings you see are clearly labeled DEMO — they're high-quality, dealer-grade examples we use for product polish and demos like this one. Real inventory comes online as we onboard dealers and brokers in private beta. We are deliberate about this: better to show you a real product with honest demo data than to fake supply we don't have."

### "What about payments — am I going to get charged?"

> "Stripe is in test mode during private beta. No real charges happen. When we flip live, every beta user gets explicit opt-in with their pricing locked at beta rates for 12 months. You will never get auto-charged without a signature."

### "Are your AI features wrapping ChatGPT?"

> "We use Anthropic Claude as our primary model and OpenAI as a fallback for resilience. We've built our own prompt pipelines, our own evaluation suite, and our own guardrails — particularly around prompt injection from user-supplied listing content. The AI is a tool we orchestrate, not a chatbot we resell. Happy to deep-dive the architecture if it's useful."

### "What integrations do you have?"

> "Live today: Stripe (payments), Supabase (auth/data), Anthropic + OpenAI (AI). Request-access tier (publicly visible, honestly gated): Plaid (bank verification), Carfax / NMVTIS (vehicle history), AircraftBlueBook / VRef (aircraft valuation), Escrow.com (escrow), DealerSocket / vAuto (DMS feeds), Uship and specialty haulers (transport), marine/auto/aviation lenders (financing), specialty insurers. Each of these flips from request-access to live when we sign the contract. You'll see the gate disappear in the UI."

### "Why should I be your first reference customer?"

> "Founder-level onboarding. Grandfathered pricing — locked at beta rates for 12 months. Direct line to me for product input — your feedback ships within the week, not the quarter. Featured placement in your vertical during launch. And a story: 'I was the first [boat dealer / aircraft broker / etc.] on TradeWind' is worth telling when your peers see us in 2027."
