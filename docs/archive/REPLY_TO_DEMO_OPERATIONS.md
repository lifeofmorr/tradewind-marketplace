# Reply-to-Demo Operations Manual

**Purpose:** Exact scripts for handling every type of reply to TradeWind outreach. No improvising — use these templates, adapt the brackets, send within 4 hours of receiving the reply.

---

## Reply Category 1: Interested / Wants to Learn More

**Signals:** "Tell me more," "This looks interesting," "How does it work," "I'd be open to a call," "Sure, let's chat."

### Response Template

```
Subject: Re: [original subject]

[First name] — great to hear from you.

I'd love to show you TradeWind in a quick 10-minute screen share — just enough
to see if it fits how you're already doing business. No pitch deck, no pressure.

Here are a few times that work this week:

- [Day], [Time] [Timezone]
- [Day], [Time] [Timezone]
- [Day], [Time] [Timezone]

Or if easier, pick a slot here: [calendar link when set up]

Either way works — just let me know what's convenient.

Don Morrison
Founder, Tradewind
[phone if comfortable sharing]
```

### After Sending
- Log in daily report: Interested → Demo Scheduling
- If no reply in 48h: send one follow-up ("Just bumping this up — would any of those times work, or should I suggest a few more?")
- If no reply after follow-up: move to "Gone quiet" — do NOT send a third chase

---

## Reply Category 2: Pricing / Cost Question

**Signals:** "How much does it cost," "What's the pricing," "Is there a free trial," "What's the catch," "Seems expensive."

### Response Template

```
Subject: Re: [original subject]

Good question, [First name].

For the private beta, I'm offering founding dealers complimentary access —
no charge for the first 60 days while we dial in the experience together.
Your feedback literally shapes the product.

After that, plans start at $149/mo for Dealer Starter (up to 25 listings,
AI tools, lead inbox). There's a Pro tier at $499/mo and a Premier tier
for high-volume dealers — but honestly, most beta partners are starting
on Starter to test the waters.

Happy to walk you through exactly what you'd get — takes about 10 minutes.
Would [Day] at [Time] work for a quick look?

Don Morrison
Founder, Tradewind
```

### After Sending
- Log in daily report: Pricing Question → answered, demo offered
- Key: never apologize for pricing. State it plainly. The 60-day free beta removes the objection.
- If they push back on post-beta pricing: "Totally fair — let's get you into the beta first and you can evaluate whether the leads and tools are worth it. No commitment beyond trying it."

## Reply Category 3: Not Interested (Polite Decline)

**Signals:** "Not for us right now," "We're good," "Maybe later," "Not a fit," "We already use [competitor]."

### Response Template

```
Subject: Re: [original subject]

Completely understand, [First name]. Thanks for letting me know.

If anything changes down the road, I'm easy to find. Wishing you
a strong [season/quarter].

Best,
Don
```

### After Sending
- Log in daily report: Not Interested — polite close
- Move to "Closed — not now" in pipeline
- Do NOT follow up again unless they reach out first
- Do NOT add to any future email sequence
- Note their objection reason for pattern tracking ("already use X," "not in market," "too early")

---

## Reply Category 4: Not Interested (Gave a Reason)

**Signals:** "We just signed with [competitor]," "We're not looking to change," "Our current system works fine," "We tried something like this before."

### Response Template

```
Subject: Re: [original subject]

Appreciate the context, [First name] — that makes sense.

No worries at all. If you ever want a second opinion on what's out there,
happy to chat informally. No agenda.

Best,
Don
```

### After Sending
- Log the specific reason — these are gold for competitive intelligence
- If they mention a competitor: note it for battlecard updates
- Move to "Closed — has alternative" in pipeline
- Calendar reminder to re-evaluate in 6 months (not to re-contact, but to check if market conditions changed)

---

## Reply Category 5: Remove Me / Unsubscribe

**Signals:** "Remove me," "Unsubscribe," "Stop emailing me," "Take me off your list," "Don't contact me again."

### Response Template

```
Subject: Re: [original subject]

Done — you've been removed and won't hear from me again.
Apologies for the interruption.

Don
```

### After Sending
- **Immediately** remove from all lists, spreadsheets, and CRM
- Add to a permanent do-not-contact list
- Log in daily report: Remove Me — processed
- **Response time SLA: within 4 hours, never longer than 24 hours**
- Never re-add this person under any circumstances

---

## Reply Category 6: Auto-Reply / Out of Office

**Signals:** "I'm out of the office until [date]," auto-generated response, vacation notice.

### Action
- Note their return date
- Set a calendar reminder to follow up 2 business days after their return
- Follow-up uses the "interested" template, adapted: "I reached out a couple weeks ago about TradeWind — wanted to circle back now that you're back. Worth a quick look?"

---

## Reply Category 7: Wrong Person / Referral

**Signals:** "I'm not the right person," "You should talk to [name]," "Let me forward this," "Try [name] instead."

### Response Template

```
Subject: Re: [original subject]

Thanks for pointing me in the right direction, [First name].
I'll reach out to [referred name] — appreciate the help.

Don
```

### After Sending
- Research the referred person before contacting
- Reference the referral: "Your colleague [First name] suggested I reach out..."
- Log original contact as "Referral source" with a positive note
- The referred contact counts as a warm lead — adjust template tone accordingly

---

## Reply Category 8: Asks a Specific Question

**Signals:** "Do you support [feature]?", "Can it do [X]?", "What about [specific need]?"

### Response Approach
- Answer the specific question directly and honestly
- If TradeWind has the feature: describe it briefly, offer to demo it
- If TradeWind doesn't have the feature: say so, explain if it's on the roadmap
- **Never lie about capabilities. Never say "coming soon" for things not planned.**

### Template

```
Subject: Re: [original subject]

Great question, [First name].

[Direct, honest answer to their specific question.]

[If yes: "Happy to show you exactly how it works — would a 10-minute
screen share on [Day] work?"]

[If no: "That's not something we support today. [If planned: 'It's on
our roadmap for Q[X].'] [If not planned: 'I appreciate you flagging it
— that's exactly the kind of input that helps us prioritize.']"]

Don
```

## Timing Rules

| Situation | Response Deadline |
|---|---|
| Interested reply | Within 4 hours (same business day) |
| Pricing question | Within 4 hours |
| Not interested | Within 24 hours |
| Remove me | Within 4 hours (hard maximum: 24 hours) |
| Out of office | Note return date, follow up return + 2 days |
| Referral | Research + send within 48 hours |
| Specific question | Within 24 hours (research if needed) |

## Tone Rules

- First name, never "Dear [Name]"
- No exclamation points in first reply (one per email maximum after that)
- No buzzwords: don't say "synergy," "disrupt," "game-changer," "revolutionary"
- No fake urgency: don't say "spots filling up," "limited time," "act now"
- No third-person self-reference: don't say "The TradeWind team" — it's just you
- Sign as "Don" — you're the founder, own it
- Match their energy: if they write two sentences, reply in four. Don't send a novel.
