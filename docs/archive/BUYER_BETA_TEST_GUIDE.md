# Buyer Beta Test Guide

You don't have to "test" anything formally — you're a real buyer, looking for a real boat or vehicle. But because this is private beta, here's a tour of the features that aren't obvious, and the parts of the experience we'd love your honest reaction to.

## The 10-minute walkthrough

1. **Browse the marketplace.** Start at `/browse`. Filter by category, state, price, condition. Sort by Deal Score to see the standouts.
2. **Open a listing.** Click into anything that catches your eye. Pay attention to:
   - **Deal Score** — our algorithmic read on whether the price is fair given specs and comparable inventory. It's guidance, not gospel.
   - **Cost to Own** — estimated 1-, 3-, and 5-year cost including insurance, fuel, maintenance, depreciation. Click the breakdown.
   - **TradeWind Advantage** badges — the listing's verified strengths (e.g., "single owner," "service records," "below market").
3. **Save a listing.** Heart icon. Saved items live at `/buyer/saved` and feed the comparison tool.
4. **Compare.** `/buyer/compare` lets you place 2–4 saved listings side by side. Specs, prices, deal scores, and cost-to-own all line up in one view.
5. **Try the offer builder.** On any "open to offers" listing, click **Build offer**. Walk through the wizard — it drafts a compelling offer message with comparable-pricing context. You can send as-is or edit.
6. **Submit a service request.** From any listing or from the dedicated landing pages:
   - `/financing` — pre-approval request.
   - `/insurance` — quote request.
   - `/inspections` — survey/inspection booking.
   - `/transport` — delivery quote.
   - `/concierge` — anything else (slip placement, registration, detailing).
7. **Check the Buy-Ready Checklist** at `/buyer`. It tracks what you've completed (saved listings, financing, insurance, inspection booked, transport quoted) so you know what's left before pulling the trigger.
8. **Visit the Trust Center** at `/trust`. Read it once. It's how we explain what's verified, what's not, and what to look out for.

## What we're hoping you'll do

- Save 3–10 listings that are actually interesting to you.
- Submit at least one real service request (financing or insurance is most common).
- Use the offer builder on at least one listing — even if you don't send it.
- Message a dealer through the platform on at least one listing you're seriously considering.

## Where to be skeptical

- **Deal Score** is algorithmic — it doesn't know that this exact unit has the rare gel coat option, or that the seller is motivated. Use it as a sanity check, not a final verdict.
- **Cost-to-Own** is modeled from regional averages. Your insurance quote and your local maintenance costs may differ materially.
- **Photos** — about 30% of beta inventory uses placeholder imagery. We're flagging those visibly. Always ask the dealer/seller for additional photos before getting serious.

## What we want to hear about

- Anything that felt confusing or made you bail.
- Any moment you wished a feature existed and it didn't.
- Any place the data felt wrong (price, specs, location, photos).
- The first thing you tried to do that we don't yet support.

## Reporting

Easiest: email the address in your welcome note with a subject line like "TradeWind feedback — [topic]." We read everything. For bugs, the `BUG_REPORT_TEMPLATE.md` makes triage faster but isn't required.

## Things that aren't done yet

See `KNOWN_LIMITATIONS.md` for the full list. The biggest one for buyers: payment flows are in Stripe test mode during beta — any "purchase" is a sandbox transaction, not a real charge.

Thanks for kicking the tires.
